Notification Eligibility 1. The students should be permanent residents of the Union Territories of Jammu and Kashmir and Ladakh. 2. Students who qualify for the national-level tests like NEET, JEE, CLAT, etc. are also eligible to apply. 3. The students must have passed class 12 from the J&K board or CBSE affiliated schools located in the Union Territories of J&K and Ladakh. 4. The students must have passed 10+3 diploma from the Union Territories of J & K and Ladakh Polytechnic for admission in the 2nd year of registration. The counseling will be held subject to the vacancy in the previous academic year in the prescribed list of Institutions on the vacant seats of the Engineering category. 5. The reservation policy will be followed by UTs of the J&K and Ladakh regions. 6. The family income of a student should not be more than Rs 8 lakh per year. 7. Students must fulfill the minimum age limit and criteria as prescribed by the Institution/University.
